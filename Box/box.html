Recycling for Boxes

Introduction:
Carton box recycling, li ke any other type of recycling, would involve breaking down the c arton box and using the material to make new cartons or other paper products. Recycling materials aims to re duce the amount of waste that goes to landfills, by reusing that would otherwise be thrown away. Recycling paper also serves to help the environment by reducing pollution and saving trees, since making new products from recycled materials requires less energy than making them from scratch

Boxes Recycling Process
The carton recycling process starts with the collection of used cartons. Once the used cartons have been collected, they are then sent to a waste arton box recycling plant. At the plant, here is what happens. The cartons are sent to a recycling machine where they are turned into pulp and the fibers separated. The mixture is then bleached using various chemicals. The fibers pass through a screening and cleaning process to remove any contaminants. The mixture is washed to ensure any remaining ink gets removed. The pulp is then pressed into new paperboard. The newly made paperboard is then shipped (in rolls) to the carton box manufacturer. The recycled paper material may also be sent to other paper co mpanies to make other products.
